About the Atlas

The Africa Building Regulations Atlas provides comprehensive data on building regulations, planning and zoning, building control, and transparency across Sub-Saharan African countries. This data is derived from the World Bank's B-READY (Business Ready) initiative.

Methodology

Data is collected through multiple sources including the B-READY framework, SSBRR (State of Sub-Saharan Building Regulatory Regimes), and GABC (Global Alliance for Buildings and Construction). The building regulations component examines building codes, planning requirements, inspection processes, and transparency mechanisms.

Data Sources:

  • BR - B-READY (Business Ready) Initiative
  • SSBRR - State of Sub-Saharan Building Regulatory Regimes
  • GABC - Global Alliance for Buildings and Construction
